Frequently Asked Questions

What is a "scrape"?
A scrape is a batch of Instagram posts processed in one session, extracting 40+ data points including engagement metrics and creator intelligence.
What's included in the free plan?
3 scrapes per day, up to 3 URLs per scrape, full analytics dashboard, creator intelligence, and 7 days data retention.
How do custom plans work?
Our team at Quantm Climb will work with you to build a tailored solution that fits your specific business requirements and scale.
What kind of data do you extract?
We extract comprehensive data including engagement metrics, follower counts, verification status, content analytics, and detailed creator profiles.
Can I export my data?
Yes! All plans include CSV export functionality for easy integration with your existing analytics workflows.
Is there an API available?
API access is available with custom enterprise plans. Contact us to discuss your integration requirements.
